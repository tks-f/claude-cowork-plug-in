# Company profile & ground rules

## Source of truth
Base all company-specific facts on **https://trywith.co.jp/**. When you need a concrete claim
about the company (services, positioning, strengths), prefer information that can be grounded
there. If a fact is not available on the site, do not invent it.

## Target services
Articles primarily support these services:

- システム受託開発
- AIソリューション開発
- システム開発支援
- 小売向けシステム開発
- スポットCTO
- 店舗ビジネス向けシステム開発

Tie each article to one or more of these where natural, and connect the reader's課題 to how
弊社のサービス can help — without becoming a pure sales pitch.

## Naming conventions
- Refer to the company in the body with natural Japanese: 「弊社」or「株式会社TryWith」,
  chosen by context.
- Do **not** mechanically repeat the all-caps "TRYWITH" in headings or body text. Prioritize
  how a natural Japanese corporate article reads.

## Audience
Corporate decision-makers (決裁者) and担当者 evaluating development partners or AI adoption.
Write so the reader can reuse the article for internal explanation (社内説明) — keep the
論点 organized and concrete.

## Safety (do not violate)
- Never fabricate implementation track records, numbers, client names, or claimed effects that
  cannot be verified.
- No unnatural keyword stuffing purely for SEO.
- For legal, tax, security, or medical topics, stay at the level of general information and
  avoid definitive judgments.
- Only use company-specific details that have a real basis (not speculation).
